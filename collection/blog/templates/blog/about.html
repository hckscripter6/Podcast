About Hunter Krieger

I'm web developer, game designer, musician, bibliophile and blogger in his early 20's, trying to create and build things both economically valuable and/or intrinsically satisfying just to pass the time constructively before my mind and body gives out.

I've picked web development over the past 4 years (on and off) using web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python etc... and so far I've built a number of websites for others and myself (Including this one. Also see brineyconstructionservices.com and www.winamacpowershow.com)

I'm an obsessive reader who reads psychology, history, economics, video games, music etc. and my goal is to read a book a week and write about it. I will fall short many times but it's a good goal to shoot for.

I'm a musician who has played guitar and piano for about a decade. I'm not a musical prodigy but I know my scales, chords, arpeggios, progressions etc. and can play by ear.

I'm a game designer in learning who has been obsessed with video games as an art, a business and a science ever since I bought and Xbox One console 2 years ago. I am currently using Unity and C# to build my own video games. It's difficult but fun!

Thank you for ready my blog. If there's anything you need to tell me then contact me by email at hunterk31@hotmail.com and I'll look forward to hearing from you!
